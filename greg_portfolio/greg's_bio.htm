Greg Haynes:
Web Developer – Graphic Designer – Interaction Designer

My name is Gregory Haynes and I have a Bachelor of Arts in Interactive Media and Journalism from DePaul University. I chose to major in those two fields because I am very passionate about technology and delivering effective communication. Currently, I'm in the process of receiving a Master of Science degree in Human Computer Interaction because I enjoy creating, designing, developing, and implementing innovative and cutting edge software programs, websites, mobile apps, computer games, etc.

     

My experience is a combination of Web Development, Web Programming, and Content Management. I have used web technologies such as HTML/XHTML/HTML5, CSS/CSS3, JavaScript/jQuery, XML, JSP, and SQL to develop landing pages, web applications, and online courses for various companies.

     

I have also used content management systems and e-commerce platforms such as Spark Enginuity, ATG Portal, Moodle, Angel, and SharePoint to update and edit content for multiple websites. With my technological and communication skills, I plan to be a valuable asset to any organization.
