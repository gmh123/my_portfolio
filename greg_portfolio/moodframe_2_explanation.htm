Objective:

My objective was to create a mood board that best represented the interface for the treadmill of RPM Fitness.

Method:

I designed everything revolving around a sense of speed including the background image, the time bar, the menu items and the runners on the interface.

Solution:

My group members and I used this idea to build a more dynamic simulation of how the interface of the treadmill would interact.
