Objective:

Based on the 7 Deadly Sins, I had to show how the film, How High, was best representative of the sin, sloth.

Method:

Like Good Luck Chuck, the color pallette I used was red, black, and white to represent how shallow and empty the film was. I decided to use a type-face called sloth with the words How High in big letters. I also made stamp effects to have that smudgy/smeary effect for the piece and topped it off with a typographic cigarette/blunt with a sloth word smoke effect.

Solution:

I was successfully able to communicate my ideas of how How High was representative of the sin, sloth.
