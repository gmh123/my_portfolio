Objective:

My objective was to assist a senior Front End Developer in creating, designing and editing HTML report mockups for the Bankers Portal Network website.

Method:

From the research we gathered on how insurance agents were having trouble navigating through online reports, we created HTML mockups from scratch and edited existing HTML mockups to bring forth a better design.

Solution:

Each of the newly designed mock–up reports were put onto the Bankers Portal Network website. Insurance Agents were now able to easily navigate through these reports.
