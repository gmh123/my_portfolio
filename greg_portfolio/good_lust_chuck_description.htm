Objective:

Based on the 7 Deadly Sins, I had to show how the film, Good Luck Chuck, was most representative of the sin lust.

Method:

With the film, Good Luck Chuck, I chose the color palette of red, white, and black in order to represent how shallow and empty this film was. I created a typographic illustration of lips and made them smudgy, full, and sloppy with lustful quotes from the film. That design was representative of how sexually sloppy and messy the film was.

Solution:

I was successfully able to communicate my ideas of how Good Luck Chuck was representative of the sin, lust.
