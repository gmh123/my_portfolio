Objective:

Based on Mos Def's song "Love", I had to create a music poster that was well–representative of not only the song, but the meaning of the song as well.

Method:

Since Mos Def's song entitled, "Love", talked more about community love rather than urban love, I decided to make more of a poster that represented a scene. I used a typographic brick wall that contained the lyrics of the song as well as graffiti like fonts to convey that urban/community atmosphere.

Solution:

I presented this poster to one of my professors and she felt that the song was very representative to Mos Def's style.
