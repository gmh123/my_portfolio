Objective:

My objective was to take the interactive map on a website (which was geared towards children) and design an easier way for them to access information. One of the issues this map had was that it displayed the search results and options in various places on the page. This made it hard for children to quickly access the information they needed.

Method:

I basically created user-friendly controls to the interactive map and the search portion of the site by allowing the user to narrow down his or her options.

Solution:

My instructor who also works with YouMedia commended my work and stated that my design strategy was another way to improve usability on the site.
